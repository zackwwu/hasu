Why This Works for Tradies

No coordinates

Front/back/left/right from the doorway perspective. Every tiler understands this intuitively — it's how they walk into a job.

Help icon for first-timers

The ! icon shows the perspective diagram once. After that, the tiler knows the convention. The mini top-down preview on the create screen serves as a quick reminder.

Toggle off unused surfaces

Not every room needs all 5 surfaces. Tap to deselect. The 3D room view will only show selected surfaces.
