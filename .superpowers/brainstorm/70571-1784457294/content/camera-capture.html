Technical Note

Uses Flutter camera plugin + edge detection. When the tiler positions the tile within the guides, edge detection finds the 4 corners of the tile (like document scanning). The captured image is perspective-corrected to a flat square using a homography transform. Result: a clean, square texture ready for rendering.
