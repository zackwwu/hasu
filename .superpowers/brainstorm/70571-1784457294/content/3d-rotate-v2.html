Why 90° Works

  • Rectangular rooms = 4 wall pairs. 90° covers them all.
  • No surface is ever hidden behind another at exactly 90°.
  • One tap per rotation. Maximum 3 taps to see any wall.
  • Same isometric math — just add 90° to the angle.
  • Top-down toggle gives the floor perspective.
