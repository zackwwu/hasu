How It Works

MVP: Discrete views

4 preset buttons at the top:
• Front-Left (default)
• Front-Right
• Back-Left
• Top-Down
Each is just a different isometric angle. Zero extra complexity — same math, different angle parameter.

Stretch: Swipe to orbit

Horizontal swipe rotates the camera around the room. Isometric angle updates on each frame. But painter's algorithm needs to reorder surfaces when one occludes another — that's the complex part.

Top-Down view

Shows floor + wall positions from above. Useful for checking floor tile layout and grout alignment. Walls appear as lines at the room perimeter.
