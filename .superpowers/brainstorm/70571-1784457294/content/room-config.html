How Positioning Works (MVP)

Auto-computed. The tiler only enters the room's overall width × depth × height. The app automatically places:

  • Front wall at z=0, facing inward, spanning full width × height
  • Back wall at z=depth, facing inward, spanning full width × height
  • Left wall at x=0, facing inward, spanning full depth × height
  • Right wall at x=width, facing inward, spanning full depth × height
  • Floor at y=0, facing up, spanning full width × depth

The tiler can then toggle off surfaces they don't need (e.g., a room with only 2 walls being tiled). For MVP, surface dimensions and positions are derived from room dimensions — not freely editable.

Stretch goal: free-form positioning where the tiler can place surfaces anywhere in 3D space, useful for L-shaped rooms or partial walls.
