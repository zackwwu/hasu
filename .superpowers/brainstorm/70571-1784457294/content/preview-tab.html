Key UX Decisions

3D is the overview

The 3D view is not just a pretty picture — it's how tilers see the whole room at once and spot issues (misaligned grout, unbalanced layout).

Tap to select, edit to refine

Tapping a surface in 3D selects it and shows an info card with "Edit Layout". This switches to the Layout tab with that surface pre-selected.

Surface selector on Layout tab

The horizontal chip strip on the Layout tab serves as secondary navigation. Primary navigation is from the 3D view.
