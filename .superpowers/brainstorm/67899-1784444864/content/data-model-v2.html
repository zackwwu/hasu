Data Model

6 entities, linked through SurfaceTileGroup

Room

idunique identifier
namee.g. "Bathroom 3F"
unitsmm or in
surfaceslist of walls and floors

Surface

typewall or floor
dimensionswidth × height (in room units)
position{x, y, z} + rotation — relative to room origin
groutcolor: black | grey | white, width: float (default 3mm)
tileAssignmentslist of SurfaceTileGroup →

SurfaceTileGroup

Links a tile group to a surface region. One wall can have 2+ of these (e.g. field tile + border).

tileGroupId→ TileGroup
region{x, y, w, h} — which part of the surface this group occupies
patterngrid | herringbone | brick | stacked
offset{x, y} — manual fine-tune drag offset
lockedbool — sync movement with other surfaces

TileGroup

namee.g. "White subway tile"
tileWidth, tileHeightsingle tile dimensions
texturePathfile path to captured/imported texture image
sourcecaptured or imported

LayoutResult

Computed tile placements. Cached — only recomputed when params change.

surfaceIdwhich surface
tileslist of {x, y, w, h, isCut, cutEdge, tileGroupId}
stalebool — true if params changed since last compute

How They Connect

Room ──▶ Surface ──▶ SurfaceTileGroup ◀── TileGroup
one-to-many many-to-many join

LayoutResult ←── computed from Surface dims + SurfaceTileGroup params + TileGroup sizes
